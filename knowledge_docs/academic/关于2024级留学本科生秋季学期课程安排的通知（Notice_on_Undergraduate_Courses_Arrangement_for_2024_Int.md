关于2024级留学本科生秋季学期课程安排的通知

各位2024级留学本科生新生：

根据学校工作安排，现将2024级留学本科生课程教学方案公布如下：

1.本学期留学本科生课程将从第三周（9月18日）开始。

2.所有2024级本科留学生一年级课程开设地点为江宁校区。

3.所有2024级本科留学生一年级课程已置入教务系统，无需选课，请根据所在专业查阅本学期课表。从二年级起，所有留学本科生与中国学生同堂上课，请联系各学院，由专业学院安排学习。各学院联系方式稍后公布。

秋季学期2024级留学本科生课程开设清单见附件1; 各专业课表见附件2；2024级留学本科生班级号见附件3；秋季学期校历见附件；4；查看课表及期末成绩查询请登陆教务系统,网址如下：<http://jwxt.hhu.edu.cn/jsxsd/framework/jsMain_hehdx.htmlx>）。

4.如果无故不参加课堂教学，未在规定时间内按要求提交课程作业或通过课程考核，则不能获得该课程的学分。

 请所有2024级留学本科生新生加入QQ群，群名：河海2024级本科留学生交流群；群号：753529593。

**Notice on Undergraduate Courses Arrangement for 2024  International Undergraduates (Autumn Semester)**

Dear New Undergraduate Students of 2024:

According to Hohai University’s arrangement, the teaching plan for the courses of 2024 international undergraduates is hereby announced as follows:

1. The courses of international undergraduate program of 2024 will start from week 3 (September 18th).

2. All first-year courses for the 2024 international undergraduates will be held at Jiangning Campus.

3. All first-year courses for 2024 international undergraduates have been set in the online system, and there is no need to select courses. Please check the course schedule according to your class number. From the second year onwards, all undergraduate students go to the same classes with Chinese students. From Year 2 onwards, please contact your college for course arrangement. The contact information of each college will be announced later.

The list of courses offered for 2024 international undergraduates in the autumn semester can be found in Attachment 1; the course schedules for each major are in Attachment 2; the class numbers for 2024 international undergraduates are in Attachment 3; the autumn semester academic calendar is in Attachment 4. To view the course schedule and final grade inquiries, please log in to the educational administration system at the following URL: <http://jwxt.hhu.edu.cn/jsxsd/framework/jsMain_hehdx.htmlx>.

 4. If you do not attend the class teaching for no reason, do not submit the course assignments as required or pass the course within the prescribed time, you will not be able to obtain the credits of the course.

All 2024 international undergraduates are invited to join the QQ group. The group number is 753529593.
