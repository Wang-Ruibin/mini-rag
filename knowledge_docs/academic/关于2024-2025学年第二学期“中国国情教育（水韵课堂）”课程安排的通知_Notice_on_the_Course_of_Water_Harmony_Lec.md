# 关于2024-2025学年第二学期“中国国情教育（水韵课堂）”课程安排的通知    Notice on the Course of Water Harmony Lectures

> 来源: https://jwc.hhu.edu.cn/2025/0319/c3115a299758/page.htm

各位港航“2+2”双学位项目留学本科生：

 “中国国情教育（水韵课堂）” （本科课程号为F2101203）是港航“2+2”双学位项目留学生的必修课，主要内容为介绍中国与水相关知识。本学期该课程设10个专题讲座，现已在“河海大学课堂在线”平台上线，每个专题讲座含有讲授视频和考试。学习课程的流程如下：

1. 3月23日前，登录教务系统注册课程（具体操作流程详见附件2）

2. 3月24日至4月25日期间，登录“河海大学课堂在线”平台（具体操作流程详见附件2）自行安排时间完成线上视频学习及对应的考试。

3. 4月30日之后，登录本科生教务系统查看成绩。成绩构成如下：总成绩=完整看完所有课程视频（40%）+完成10个专题讲座的考试（60%）。成绩大于等于60分计为通过。

教务处

2025年3月18日

**Notice on the Course of Water Harmony Lectures**

Dear undergraduate students in the “2+2” Dual Degree Program in Port and Waterway Engineering：

The Course of Water Harmony Lectures (F2101203), is a compulsory course required in the program for all international students of “2+2” Dual Degree Program in Port and Waterway Engineering. The main content is to introduce China's water and related situations. In this semester, the course has 10 special lectures, all of which have been launched on the Online Teaching Platform of Hohai University. Each special lecture contains teaching videos and exam. The procedure to take this course is as follows:

1. Before March 23, log in to the undergraduate system to register for courses (see Attachment 2 for the specific operation procedure)

2. From March 24 to April 25, log in to the “Online Teaching Platform of Hohai University” (see Attachment 2 for the specific operation procedure) and arrange time to complete online learning and exams.

3. After April 30, log in to the undergruate system to view the results. The grade composition is as follows: Total grade = Watching all course videos in full (40%) + Completing the exams for 10 special lectures (60%). A grade of 60 or above is considered passing.

 

Attachments: 1. Introduction of Water Harmony Lectures

2. Instruction of Online Teaching Platform of HohaiUniversity
