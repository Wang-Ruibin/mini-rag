# 关于2025级留学本科生春季学期课程安排的通知 Notice on Undergraduate Courses Arrangement for 2025  International Undergraduates (Spring Semester)

> 来源: https://jwc.hhu.edu.cn/2026/0227/c3115a323183/page.htm

各位2025级留学本科生新生：

根据学校工作安排，现将2025级留学本科生课程教学方案公布如下：

1.本学期留学本科生课程将从第一周（3月2日）开始。

2.所有2025级本科留学生一年级课程开设地点为江宁校区。

3.所有2025级本科留学生一年级课程已置入教务系统，无需选课，请根据所在专业查阅本学期课表。从二年级起，所有留学本科生与中国学生同堂上课，请联系各学院，由专业学院安排学习。

春季学期2025级留学本科生课程开设清单见附件1; 各专业课表见附件2；2025级留学本科生班级号见附件3；春季学期校历见附件4；各学院联系方式见附件5。查看课表及期末成绩查询请登陆教务系统, 网址如下：https://jwxt.hhu.edu.cn/jsxsd/sso.jsp）。账号为学号，密码与河海大学信息门户密码一致。

4.如果无故不参加课堂教学，未在规定时间内按要求提交课程作业或通过课程考核，则不能获得该课程的学分。

教务处

2026年2月27日

**Notice on Undergraduate Courses Arrangement for 202****5****International** **Undergraduates****(S****pring****Semester)**

Dear New Undergraduate Students of 2025:

According to Hohai University’s arrangement, the teaching plan for the courses of 2025 international undergraduates is hereby announced as follows:

1. The courses of international undergraduate program of 2025 will start from week 1 (March 2nd).

2. All first-year courses for the 2025 international undergraduates will be held at Jiangning Campus.

3.  All first-year courses for 2025 international undergraduates have been set in the online system, and there is no need to select courses. Please check the course schedule according to your class number. From the second year onwards, all undergraduate students go to the same classes with Chinese students. From Year 2 onwards, please contact your college for course arrangement. 

The list of courses offered for 2025 international undergraduates in the spring semester can be found in Attachment 1; the course schedules for each major are in Attachment 2; the class numbers for 2025 international undergraduates are in Attachment 3; the spring semester academic calendar is in Attachment 4; the contact of college teaching secretary for undergraduate students is in Attachment 5. To view the course schedule and final grade inquiries, please log in to the educational administration system at the following URL: https://jwxt.hhu.edu.cn/jsxsd/sso.jsp.

The account is your student ID, and the password is the same as the one for Hohai University Information Portal.

 4. If you do not attend the class teaching for no reason, do not submit the course assignments as required or pass the course within the prescribed time, you will not be able to obtain the credits of the course.
