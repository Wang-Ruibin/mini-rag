 如何获取学信网图像采集码  
How to get the Student Status Info QR Code for Degree Photo Taking 
 
Important: The photo will be an important part of the education certificate in the Ministry of Education's registration platform. 
Photos (both paper and electronic versions) will be used for the graduation certificate.

CHSI  APP
