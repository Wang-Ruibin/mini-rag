 如何拍摄照片  
How to shoot the photo online  
 
1. Login to WeChat  
2. Search and open the mini program “ 江苏
图采” , or follow the wechat official account  
“江苏省招就中心 ”, choose the item “图像
采集”  
 
 
  
 3. Scan or upload the personal QR code to log in. After the name verification is passed, 
follow the steps.  
 
4. Type  your Chinese  phone number  
 5. Read the university ’s notice  
 6. Reading Precautions for Photo Shooting  
 
 
7. Select to receive notifications:  
It is suggested that students choose to receive photo status notification and payment result 
notification so as to know their progress in time.  
  
 
  8. Select the requirements for on -site 
shooting, and click “ Next ” to enter the 
shooting. Only rear camera shooting is allowed here (you need to complete watching the video before clicking Next):  
    


2 
 9. Click “Next ” to enter the shooting interface.  Try to place in the 
viewfinder.  
 10. After clicking "Start shooting", the photo preview will be displayed. 
If there is no problem, click "OK" for  photo making 
 
 
11. After the production is completed, the 
system will automatically test, if  pass, the 
photo will be  automatically stored in the 
draft box,  if not pass, please read the 
corresponding message and back to re-take.  
   
  
  
 
 
 
 12. In case of multiple failure s in shooting, 
the account will be locked, please contact academic office to unlock with by email, (ie_academic@hhu.edu.cn) with the 
following content: “ Certificate photo 
account unlock + Student ID number ”. The 
locked message is shown as following.  
 
 
  


3 
 13. In case of multiple failures of other 
reasons,  the account will be locked and you 
need to wait for some time then restart. The 
locked message is shown as following.  
 
 
    
  14. Draft box photos can be deleted after 24 
hour. If the box is full, you can’t shoot. The 
message is shown as following.  
 
 
  
 
   
 
 
 15. After passing the detection, click Next to 
enter the step of uploading , where you can 
also choose to return to the draft box and select other photos of the draft box.  
 
16. After uploading, the system will enter the last page to pay by 
WeChat in the system.  
 
  
 
 
 17. Photo download page. If the system has been completed, you can 
download and save the photo.
